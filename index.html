Our Services

Call Us

Structural Engeneering Design & Documentation

Structural engineering services across all stages of development including Concept Design, Development Approval (DA), Building Approval (BA), Design & Construct, and Construction. All works are compliant with the NCC and relevant Australian Standards. We specialise in structural steel, timber, and reinforced concrete, including precast concrete systems such as tilt-up construction.

Structural Inspections & Certifications (Form 15 & Form 12)

Mandatory structural inspections, engineering certification, and compliance sign-off during construction.

New builds, Extensions & Renovations

Specialists in new developments, extensions, renovations, house raising and building-under works, and additional storeys over existing structures.

Retrospective Inspections, Remedial Works and Certification

Assessment of existing structures to identify defects and non-compliances, with engineering solutions, remedial specifications, and certification where applicable.

Forensic Investigation and Reports

Independent forensic investigation of structural failures, defects, and damage, supported by clear engineering analysis and expert reporting.
